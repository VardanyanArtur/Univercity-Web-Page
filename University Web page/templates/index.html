Welcome To UFAR

The French University in Armenia (UFAR) was established in 2000 under a cultural, scientific and technical cooperation agreement signed in 1995 between the French and Armenian governments.The value of integrity was at the very foundation of the creation of UFAR. The university considers it necessary to develop and ensure a culture of integrity. As soon as they enter the University, students know that impartial assessment, examination monitoring and disciplinary committees make UFAR an institution of integrity. This integrity is all the more important as students appropriate it and become its bearers in their daily actions and in their professional lives. Indeed, integrity is implemented in UFAR procedures but also in concrete projects with a strong ethical connotation. The university's desire is indeed to embody this value of integrity through concrete actions. This is how UFAR has set up a Plan for the Reintegration of Student Soldiers (PRES) and a program to help students with academic difficulties upon returning from military service, the PREM'S (Plan for the Reintegration of Ex-Military Students towards Success). This allows us to show examples of integrity to students to encourage them to continue to embody this value as ambassadors of UFAR.
